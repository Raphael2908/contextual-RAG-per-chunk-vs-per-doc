# Commercial Office Lease

This Commercial Office Lease (the "Lease") is entered into as of 1 March 2024
between Harbor Point Properties LLC ("Landlord") and Vela Robotics Inc
("Tenant").

## 1. Premises

1.1 Landlord leases to Tenant approximately 8,500 rentable square feet on the
fourth floor of the building located at 220 Wharf Street, Suite 400 (the
"Premises").

## 2. Term

2.1 The term of this Lease is five (5) years, commencing on 1 April 2024 (the
"Commencement Date") and expiring on 31 March 2029, unless sooner terminated.

2.2 Tenant has one option to renew for an additional three (3) years at the
then-prevailing fair market rent, exercisable on not less than nine (9) months'
written notice prior to expiration.

## 3. Rent

3.1 Base rent is forty-two dollars ($42.00) per rentable square foot per year,
payable in equal monthly installments in advance on the first day of each month.

3.2 Base rent increases by three percent (3%) on each anniversary of the
Commencement Date.

3.3 Tenant shall pay its proportionate share of operating expenses and real estate
taxes as additional rent ("CAM charges").

## 4. Security Deposit

4.1 Upon execution, Tenant shall deposit with Landlord a security deposit equal to
two months' base rent, to be held as security for Tenant's performance.

## 5. Use

5.1 The Premises shall be used solely for general office and research and
development purposes and for no other use without Landlord's prior written consent.

## 6. Maintenance and Repairs

6.1 Landlord shall maintain the building structure, roof, and common areas. Tenant
shall maintain the interior of the Premises in good condition, ordinary wear and
tear excepted.

## 7. Assignment and Subletting

7.1 Tenant shall not assign this Lease or sublet any part of the Premises without
Landlord's prior written consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.

## 8. Governing Law

8.1 This Lease is governed by the laws of the State of California.
