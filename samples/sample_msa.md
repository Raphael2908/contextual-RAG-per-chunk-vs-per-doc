# Master Services Agreement

This Master Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is made as of 15 January 2025
(the "Effective Date") between Northwind Software LLC ("Provider") and
Crestline Manufacturing Corp ("Client").

## 1. Services

1.1 Provider shall perform the software development and integration services
described in one or more Statements of Work ("SOW") executed under this Agreement.

1.2 Each SOW is governed by this Agreement; in the event of conflict, the SOW
controls only as to the specific services it describes.

## 2. Fees and Payment

2.1 Client shall pay Provider the fees set out in each SOW. Unless an SOW states
otherwise, fees are billed monthly in arrears.

2.2 Provider shall submit invoices on the first business day of each month, and
Client shall pay each undisputed invoice within thirty (30) days of receipt.

2.3 Late payments accrue interest at one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month
or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is lower.

## 3. Intellectual Property

3.1 All deliverables created specifically for Client under an SOW ("Work Product")
are works made for hire and, upon full payment, are owned exclusively by Client.

3.2 Provider retains ownership of its pre-existing tools, libraries, and know-how
("Provider Background IP") and grants Client a perpetual, non-exclusive license to
use Provider Background IP solely as embedded in the Work Product.

## 4. Term and Termination

4.1 This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until terminated.

4.2 Either party may terminate for convenience on sixty (60) days' written notice;
either party may terminate immediately for the other's material breach not cured
within fifteen (15) days of written notice.

## 5. Limitation of Liability

5.1 Except for breaches of confidentiality or IP obligations, each party's
aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by
Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim, and in no event exceed five
million dollars ($5,000,000).

5.2 Neither party is liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

## 6. Warranty

6.1 Provider warrants that the services will be performed in a professional and
workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards for a period of ninety (90)
days following delivery.

## 7. Governing Law

7.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.
